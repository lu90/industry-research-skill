# Research Plan

Use WBS to turn the request into research tasks.

## Standard WBS

### Macro Research

- Policy and regulation.
- Economic cycle, income, rates, inflation, trade, finance.
- Social and cultural change.
- Technology change.

### Meso Research

- Industry definition and boundary.
- Market size and growth.
- Industry map and value chain.
- Competitive structure.
- Lifecycle stage.
- Seven core modules.
- Prosperity indicators.
- Valuation logic.
- Profitability and profit-pool structure.

### Micro Research

Use only when the request includes a company, product, project, or representative player:

- Business model.
- Product/service position.
- Customer groups.
- Financial indicators.
- Operating indicators.
- Competitive comparison.
- SWOT or portfolio analysis when appropriate.

## Task Selection

- Industry overview: run macro + meso tasks.
- Industry-specific question: run the industry overview base, then add issue-tree tasks.
- Company/product analysis: run the industry overview base, then add micro tasks.

## Plan Output

The report does not need to show the full WBS unless useful. Internally, use it to decide what information to collect and what references to read.
