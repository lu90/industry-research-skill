# Information Sources

Use this file during information collection. Select sources by the question being verified, not by convenience.

## Source Priority

| Priority | Source type | Use for | Caution |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Official statistics, laws, regulatory filings, company filings | Facts, market size, policy, financials | Check date, scope, and definitions. |
| 2 | Industry associations, exchanges, reputable international agencies | Industry data and structure | May use industry-friendly definitions. |
| 3 | Research reports, consulting reports, broker reports | Frameworks, opinions, forecasts, comparable indicators | Mark as opinion unless backed by primary data. |
| 4 | News, expert interviews, communities | Signals, cases, qualitative texture | Cross-check; avoid using as sole evidence. |
| 5 | Social media and informal sources | Weak signals, user sentiment | Never treat as verified fact without support. |

## Official and Regulatory Sources

Use national/local statistics bureaus, industry regulators, ministries, exchanges, securities regulators, customs, tax, patent, standards, courts, and international agencies such as IEA, World Bank, IMF, OECD, WTO, UN agencies, WHO, or FAO.

## Company and Capital Market Sources

Use prospectus, annual/interim/quarterly reports, listed-company announcements, investor presentations, earnings calls, bond prospectuses, product documentation, pricing pages, and official customer cases.

## Research Reports and Industry Materials

Use broker industry research, consulting reports, industry association reports, third-party data platform reports, company white papers, academic papers, and think-tank reports. Extract underlying facts separately from author opinions.

## Databases and Tools

Use Wind, Choice, iFinD, CEIC, Bloomberg, Capital IQ, Factiva, 慧博投研, 萝卜投研, 天眼查, 企查查, IT 桔子, and 36 氪创投平台 when available.

## Academic and Professional Communities

Use CNKI, Wanfang, VIP, Google Scholar, Baidu Scholar, JSTOR, ResearchGate, Elsevier, 经管之家, 丁香园, GitHub, CSDN, StackOverflow, V2EX, 掘金, 聚宽, 掘金量化, and BigQuant.

## First-Hand Research

Use expert interviews, customer interviews, channel checks, store/field observation, product trials, and supplier/distributor conversations when desk research is insufficient.

## Source Use Rules

- Clarify data definition.
- Clarify source standpoint and incentive.
- Cross-verify important claims.
- Mark source date and geographic scope.
- Do not mix data with different口径 unless the report explains the adjustment.
